The Project

  • Your objective is to find a client who is looking for some software to be made to sponsor your project. Once you achieve this, everything else is largely the same as it was in CSC131.
  • Groups can choose their members until the end of the first day. Any students not in a group by the end of the day will be automatically assigned to a group with open slots.
  • Choose your groups extremely wisely. Preferably with members you've worked well with in the past and performed well on other projects. It is imperative to vet your group members to ensure they pull their weight.
  • The project will span across two semesters, of which this course is the first of two. Your main goal with CSC190 is to secure a client and flesh out your software as much as possible.
  • Your goal in part two (CSC191) will be to finalize your project and test/debug it to ensure quality.
